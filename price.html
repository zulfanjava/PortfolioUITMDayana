Team work

Within my department, the significance of teamwork is evident, especially when it comes to tasks such as planning and control. I observe the Planning Department staff executing their responsibilities seamlessly due to a strong sense of team spirit. They engage in discussions to address pending tasks collaboratively. Adopting their proactive approach has taught me the importance of being a more efficient individual and fostering a willingness to assist others in completing tasks.

Software skills

Within the Urban Development Planning Department at the Kangar Municipal Council, the utilization of computer applications holds significant importance for the department's staff. Throughout my industrial training in this department, I received instruction on utilizing Map Info as an alternative tool for locating land parcels and determining land use in Perlis. Beside that, I was consistently assigned tasks involving the use of Excel software as an alternative for inputting crucial data. Furthermore, this industrial training provided me with a clearer understanding of utilizing PowerPoint software as an alternative method for creating visually appealing presentation slides within the department. In addition, other software such as arcGIS and autoCAD are also learned and used to view plans for projects applying for planning permission.

Critical Thinking

Throughout my industrial training with the Kangar Municipal Council, I actively participated in various meetings, including the OSC Meeting. Engaging in these meetings highlighted the crucial role of team discussions in resolving issues within each department of the Kangar Municipal Council. The OSC meeting encompasses several technical departments, such as JKR, Engineering Department, Building Department, PlanMalaysia, and Urban Development Planning Department. During these sessions, departmental directors present issues, leading to discussions with superiors to reach a consensus between the YDP and the respective department. This collaborative approach underscores the significance of teamwork in addressing departmental challenges.

Communication skills

Effective communication skills play a crucial role in my department. Whenever the office phone rings and departmental staff are unavailable, I take the initiative to answer the phone and interact with customers, providing them with information about the status of their requested Planning Permission application. Additionally, I have actively engaged in numerous site visits alongside departmental staff. These visits, often conducted in collaboration with external developers, have provided me with valuable opportunities to enhance my communication skills. I have interacted with developers, expressing my opinions and contributing to the discourse during these site visits.
