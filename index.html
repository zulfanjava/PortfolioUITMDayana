About Me

Nur Dayana Hazwani Binti Dahalan

My name is Dayana Hazwani Binti Dahalan. I am a Bachelor's student in Urban and Regional Planning at UiTM Puncak Alam. I have undergone an internship at Kangar Municipal Council, Perlis for 4 months. During this industrial training, it helped me to further develop my level of knowledge regarding the planning system in Perlis. Next, it can also improve my communication skills and strengthen my level of discipline. Throughout my industrial training at the Kangar Municipal Council, I have learned to be punctual and responsible in completing assigned tasks. While I was at the Kangar Municipal Council, I was exposed to many important tasks and this gave me new knowledge and experience. I conclude that the job of an urban planner in PBT is very different from the private sector because at the Kangar Municipal Council, I was assigned to do planning control and this is very different from what I learned at Uitm.
