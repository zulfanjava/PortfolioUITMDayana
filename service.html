EXPERIENCE DEALING WITH ARCHITECTS AND CLIENTS FOR THE CONSTRUCTION PROJECT AT CHUPING, PERLIS

The site visit at the Chuping Valley Industrial Area involved four departments from the Kangar Municipal Council, namely the Urban Development Planning Department, the Building Department, the Landscape Department and the Engineering Department. This development project gave me experience dealing with developers and customers. I have been assigned to measure the area of ​​the parking lot to ensure that the parking lot is provided according to the conditions that have been set. I also had the opportunity to participate in a discussion session between the Development Department, Developers and Customers regarding the building structure in the area.

EXPERIENCE WITH THE STAFF OF THE PLANNING DEPARTMENT AND OTHER DEPARTMENTS IN MPKANGAR

As an intern in the Planning Department, I gained valuable networking experience by collaborating closely with departmental staff. This opportunity allowed me to engage in meaningful interactions, exchange ideas, and establish connections within the professional sphere. Through my internship, I enhanced my understanding of the department's dynamics and developed relationships that contributed to a rich and collaborative learning environment.

NETWORKING EXPERIENCE WITH STUDENT FROM UITM PUNCAK ALAM

During my internship, I had the opportunity to network with students from UiTM Puncak Alam who approached the Planning Department for data collection purposes. Engaging with these students allowed me to build connections, share insights, and collaborate on gathering valuable information. This experience enhanced my interpersonal skills and provided a platform for collaborative learning between interns and students from UiTM Puncak Alam.
