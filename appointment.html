MOHD IZUL FIKRI BIN ADUMI

Throughout my industrial training at the Kangar Municipal Council, I found a remarkable role model in my supervisor, Mr. Mohd Izul Fikri bin Adumi. I deeply admire him for his strong sense of responsibility and dedication to the tasks assigned to him. His commitment is evident in the thorough execution of his duties. Not only is he responsible, but he also exudes an active and cheerful demeanor when handling tasks. Despite being a planning officer in the Urban Development Planning Department, he actively participates in various programs organized by the Kangar Municipal Council. I've observed that he excels in managing his time efficiently. Despite his busy schedule in the office, he makes a point to allocate time for personal pursuits. Additionally, he demonstrates genuine concern for the well-being of the staff in the Urban Development Planning Department and practical students undergoing industrial training at the Kangar Municipal Council. His consistent care and guidance reflect his desire for me to smoothly adapt to the Kangar Municipal Council environment during my industrialÂ training.

ACHIEVEMENT

For the year 2023, the Kangar Municipal Council has received a special award from the Malaysian Institute of Planners due to the achievements made by the Department of Urban Development Planning at the Kangar Municipal Council. The Kampung Warna Warni project is a program to transform the fishing village in Seberang Ramai, Kuala Perlis as a tourist attraction in Perlis. The original purpose of this transformation program was to beautify the village but it has turned into a tourist attraction in the state of Perlis. Mr. Mohd Izul Fikri Bin Adumi, as a planning officer at the Kangar Municipal Council, has played a role and been involved in this project. He has given a high commitment to make this project a success and this award received is the best achievement for him and the Kangar Municipal Council.
